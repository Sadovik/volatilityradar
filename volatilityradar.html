Volatility Radar Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Volatility Radar handles data and protects your privacy while you use the application.

Key Points

  • No personal data collected — only on-device UserDefaults preferences are used.
  • Third parties involved: RapidAPI/Yahoo Finance (no user data sent), Apple StoreKit (handles payments), and local APNs only.
  • CCPA compliant by design — nothing to disclose, delete, or sell.
  • Children's privacy protection — fully compliant with Apple's developer guidelines.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Volatility Radar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app only stores localized user preferences and settings directly on your device using UserDefaults. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to external servers.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide core app functionality, Volatility Radar utilizes the following third-party services:

  • RapidAPI / Yahoo Finance: Used to fetch financial market data. No user data, personal identifiers, or device information are ever sent to these services.
  • Apple StoreKit: Handles in-app purchases and subscriptions securely. All payment processing is managed entirely by Apple, and the app never sees or stores your financial information.
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs): Used locally on your device to manage alerts. We do not maintain any external servers to track your notifications.

3. CCPA & CCPA/CPRA Compliance

Because Volatility Radar does not collect, track, store, or share any personal information, we are fully compliant with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) by design. There is no personal data for us to disclose, delete, or sell to third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

Volatility Radar does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or under the relevant age in your jurisdiction), in full compliance with COPPA and Apple App Store Review Guidelines. Since the app gathers no personal data from any user, no children's data is ever collected or processed.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
